Our Mission  
Cartier’s mission is to create timeless, exceptional jewelry and watchmaking pieces that embody elegance, innovation, and craftsmanship. The brand is dedicated to preserving its heritage of luxury while continuously pushing the boundaries of design and artistry.  
Events 
In July 2024, Cartier's parent company, Richemont, announced a reshuffling of its top management team. Louis Ferla, previously the chief executive of Vacheron Constantin, was appointed as the new CEO of Cartier, succeeding Cyrille Vigneron. This move is part of Richemont's broader succession planning strategy.
News 
From April 12 to November 16, 2025, the Victoria and Albert Museum in London will host a major Cartier exhibition. This showcase will feature over 350 pieces, including precious jewels, historic gemstones, iconic watches, and clocks, charting the evolution of Cartier's legacy in art, design, and craftsmanship since the early 20th century. Tickets are priced at £27.00 on weekdays and £29.00 on weekends, with concessions available.
